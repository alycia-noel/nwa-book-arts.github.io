Making Fools Tools

Date: December 12, 2025

Time: 4:30 PM - 6:00 PM

Location: Sally's House

About This Workshop

Sally introduced the group to what fools tools are and how to make them. Each person then was able to try their hand at making a fools tool.

What We Covered

  • What fools tools are
  • Techniques and materials for making fools tools: brass rod, hammer, and anvil.
  • Hands-on practice making fools tools
